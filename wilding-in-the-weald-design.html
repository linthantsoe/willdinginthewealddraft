Wilding in the Weald

Welcome to New Coghurst, a family-run farm, rewilding project and outdoor education hub, situated in a Sussex Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty. We offer a broad range of experiences for all ages, facilitating connection with nature and local history.

New Coghurst is also a Bronze Age archaeological site of significance, which is currently under excavation. There are so many ways to get involved, from archaeological digs and ancestral skills workshops to kids nature connection days and much, much more. We look forward to meeting you soon!

What We Offer

There is so much on offer at New Coghurst for people of all ages, from archaeological digs and workshops to kids nature connection days and one-to one therapeutic sessions. Click on a section to find out more.

Workshops

Primitive workshops - Try your hand at flint knapping, foraging, basket making, metal work and more

Ritual Landscapes

Rediscover the ancient practice of celebrating and honouring nature's seasonal cycles with our ritual retreat days

Outdoor activity days for families and children. Facilitating learning through imagination and play
